About Us

The Clearwater Foundation is dedicated to bringing clean and safe drinking water to underserved communities in India. We work tirelessly to implement sustainable water solutions and educate communities about water conservation.

Our Projects

Water Wells in Rural Villages

Our initiative to build water wells in remote villages has significantly improved access to clean water.

Rainwater Harvesting Systems

Installing rainwater harvesting systems helps communities collect and store water for the dry season.
